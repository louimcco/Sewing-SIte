Welcome to Sewing Maths!



A website for all of your mathematical sewing needs.

One thing that no one seems to know about sewing is the amount of maths involved. From sewing an even seam to pattern alterations, getting the maths right is essential for any sewing project. Therefore, I hope that this website can serve to make the process a little easier, where possible, by offering tools and guidance.

Happy sewing!
