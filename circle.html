What is a Circle Skirt?



A full circle skirt is a skirt that, when laid flat, forms a circle.

This kind of skirt is great for creating lots of movement and volume. Perfect for twirling!

Entering your measurements into the calculator will produce a "waist radius", as depicted in the diagram, and a "circle radius" which is the waist radius plus the skirt length.
